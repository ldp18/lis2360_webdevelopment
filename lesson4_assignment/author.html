About the Author

Lauren Proce

Lauren Proce has experienced Alzheimer's first hand. Her grandmother was diagnosed back in 2013 and passed away from the disease in October of 2018. They were both very close to eachother and she wants to raise awareness for the disease. Lauren is an Information, Communication, and Technology major at The Florida State University. She hopes to use her major towards the medical field one day.
